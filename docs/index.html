How This Works: Population-Level Risk Assessment

This tool uses machine learning trained on CDC population health data to estimate the prevalence of anxiety/depression symptoms among people with similar demographics to you. The model analyzes patterns across demographic groups to predict what percentage of people with your characteristics typically experience these symptoms.
